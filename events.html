Solution for Events

IronWifi offers solutions tailored to events such as music festivals, sporting events, and conventions. Event organizers can provide reliable, secure, high-performance Wi-Fi connectivity for attendees while gathering valuable data insights.

The Solution: Guest Wi-Fi


How it Works: IronWifi offers a comprehensive solution for event organizers to enhance their guest Wi-Fi experience while ensuring security, ease of management, and valuable insights. With IronWifi, organizers can provide seamless connectivity for guests, gather valuable data, and implement advanced security measures.


Seamless Attendee Connectivity

  • High-Density Network Design: IronWifi's network architecture is optimized for high-density environments. This ensures that large numbers of attendees can simultaneously connect to the Wi-Fi network without experiencing slow speeds or connectivity issues.
  • Customized Splash Pages: Create branded splash pages that welcome attendees and provide event information upon connection. This creates a positive first impression and sets the tone for a seamless event experience.
  • Multiple SSIDs and Access Plans: Implement multiple SSIDs (network names) to segment attendees based on their roles or access needs. Provide different access plans, such as basic browsing, premium high-speed, and VIP options, catering to diverse attendee requirements.


Advanced Security Measures

  • Secure User Authentication: IronWifi supports secure authentication methods such as WPA2 encryption and captive portal logins. This ensures that attendees' data and online activities remain protected from potential threats.
  • Guest Isolation: Implement guest isolation to prevent attendees from accessing each other's devices, enhancing security within the event network.
  • Content Filtering: Enforce content filtering to restrict access to malicious websites and inappropriate content, maintaining a secure browsing environment.

Data Collection and Insights

  1. Attendee Analytics: Gather valuable insights into attendee behavior, preferences, and movement patterns. This data can be utilized to enhance event planning, optimize traffic flow, and improve the overall attendee experience.
  2. Marketing Opportunities: Utilize captive portal pages to promote event sponsors, exhibitors, and upcoming sessions, enhancing sponsor visibility and attendee engagement.
  3. Integration with CRM Systems: Integrate IronWifi data with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems to create personalized post-event communication strategies and nurture long-term relationships with attendees.

Centralized Management

Cloud-Based Control: IronWifi's cloud-based management platform allows event organizers to remotely manage and monitor the network across various event locations and access points from a single interface.


  • Live Support: IronWifi provides 24/7 live technical support during the event, ensuring a seamless Wi-Fi experience for both attendees and event organizers.
  • Scalability: IronWifi's solution is scalable, accommodating events of varying sizes and complexity, from small conferences to large-scale music festivals and sporting events.
  • Data Retention and Compliance: IronWifi adheres to data retention and privacy regulations, ensuring that attendee data is handled responsibly and in compliance with relevant laws.
