Solutions for your Business

IronWiFi has solutions for all businesses, small to enterprise. Let Ironwifi help secure your companies Wi-Fi and protect your valuable data.

IronWifi offers robust solutions to secure business employee networks using WPA Enterprise authentication. With IronWifi, businesses can ensure strong network security while providing flexibility for various employee devices and existing authorization methods. Here's a solutions page outlining how IronWifi can effectively secure business employee networks:


WPA Enterprise Authentication


  • Robust Security: WPA Enterprise provides a higher level of security than traditional WPA2/WPA3 Pre-Shared Key (PSK) authentication methods. Each user is provided with a unique authentication credential, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access.
  • User-Specific Credentials: IronWifi empowers businesses to create individual local usernames and passwords through the IronWifi console. This approach ensures that only authorized employees can access the network.
  • Integration with Existing Methods: For seamless integration with existing authorization methods such as Azure, Okta, or Google. IronWifi supports Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) authentication. This allows employees to use their existing credentials to access the network.


SAML Authentication for Employee-Owned Devices


  • Flexibility: IronWifi's SAML integration enables employees to use their own devices while utilizing their existing credentials for network access. This reduces the need for multiple sets of credentials and enhances user convenience.
  • Streamlined Onboarding: Employees can seamlessly access the network with their familiar SAML credentials, streamlining the onboarding process and enhancing user satisfaction.


SCEP for Employer-Provided Devices


  • Strong Device Authentication: IronWifi supports Simple Certificate Enrollment Protocol (SCEP), allowing businesses to issue digital certificates to employee devices such as computers and phones. These certificates ensure strong device-level authentication.
  • How SCEP Works: A SCEP server generates and manages digital certificates for authorized devices. When an employee device connects to the network, it presents the digital certificate as a form of authentication. This method is particularly useful for businesses that provide devices to their employees.


Centralized Management


  • IronWifi Console: Businesses can manage and configure user authentication, access policies, and security settings through the intuitive IronWifi console. This centralized management streamlines network administration and ensures consistent security policies.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: IronWifi provides real-time monitoring of network activity, allowing businesses to detect and respond to any suspicious behavior or potential security threats promptly.


Technical Support and Maintenance


  • Expert Support: IronWifi offers expert technical support to assist businesses in setting up and maintaining their secure employee networks. This ensures a smooth implementation process and ongoing network reliability.
  • Data Retention and Compliance: IronWifi adheres to data retention and privacy regulations, safeguarding employee data and ensuring compliance with relevant laws.


IronWifi provides a comprehensive solution for businesses seeking to secure their employee networks using WPA Enterprise authentication. Whether through local usernames and passwords, SAML integration, or SCEP for employer-provided devices, IronWifi ensures a strong network security foundation. With centralized management, real-time monitoring, and expert support, businesses can confidently provide secure and convenient network access to their employees while protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access.
